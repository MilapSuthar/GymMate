## 1. BRAND IDENTITY

### Who You Are
You are an aesthetic bodybuilder who documents the pursuit of the ideal physique — the training, the discipline, the lifestyle, and the results. Your content blends real gym footage, honest progress, and practical knowledge to inspire people who want to build a body that looks as good as it performs.

### Your Unique Angle
The aesthetic bodybuilding space is full of either:
- Unreachable genetics-blessed influencers who don't explain their methods, or
- Pure educators who are too dry and don't show results

**You are both.** You have the physique *and* you pull back the curtain on exactly how you built it. No mystery. No gatekeeping. Just the real process.

### Brand Positioning Statement
*"I show you what an aesthetic physique actually takes — the training, the food, the consistency — and I document every step of building mine."*

### Tone of Voice
- Confident, not arrogant
- Aspirational, but grounded in reality
- Direct and no-BS
- Occasionally motivational, never preachy
- Knowledgeable but not overly scientific

### Visual Identity Direction
- **Color palette:** Black, white, and one accent (gold, red, or electric blue work well for bodybuilding brands)
- **Editing style:** Clean cuts, fast-paced gym clips, good lighting (natural or studio)
- **Thumbnail style (YouTube):** High-contrast, your physique visible, bold 2-3 word text
- **Grid style (Instagram):** Alternating gym content + lifestyle, cohesive warm or dark tone
